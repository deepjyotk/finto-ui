## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Finto a broker or financial advisor?**
No. Finto is a portfolio intelligence platform. We don't execute trades or provide investment advice—we help you analyze and understand your portfolio through AI-powered insights.

**How do I add my portfolio to Finto?**
You can upload your holdings by uploading Excel or CSV files from your broker. Finto supports multiple brokers including Zerodha, AngelOne, and Grow.

**How does the AI portfolio analysis work?**
Simply ask questions about your portfolio in natural language. Our AI assistant analyzes your holdings, calculates metrics, and provides instant answers. You can ask about profit/loss, risk metrics, allocations, or any custom analysis.

**What kind of portfolio questions can I ask?**
You can ask questions about profit and loss, risk metrics (volatility, drawdown), performance comparisons, sector allocations, individual stock analysis, or request custom calculations. The AI generates calculations on-demand to answer your specific questions.

**Is my financial data secure?**
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and secure connections. Your data stays private and is never shared with third parties.

**Can I access real-time market data?**
Yes. Finto integrates with financial data sources to provide live prices, financial statements, earnings data, and market news from NSE, BSE, and SEBI sources.
